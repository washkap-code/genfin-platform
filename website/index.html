About GENFIN

One of Zimbabwe's most active healthcare funders

GENFIN Medical Aid Fund is a health insurance company fully registered with the Ministry of Health and Child Welfare, and a member of the Association of Healthcare Funders of Zimbabwe.

  • Registered & accredited with the Ministry of Health and Child Welfare and AHFOZ.
  • In-house medical services — optical, GP and dental care at our own clinic.
  • Nationwide medication delivery to homes, schools and companies across the country.
  • Corporate social responsibility through free healthcare wellness programs.
