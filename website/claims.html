What you can claim

Your claimable benefits depend on your plan. All claims below are available on Gold and Platinum.

General practitioner

GP consultations, follow-ups, and chronic disease management visits. Up to 6 covered visits per year on the Vital scheme, unlimited on Gold and Diamond.

Specialist referrals

Cardiologists, orthopaedic surgeons, dermatologists, and more — covered on referral from your GP. Prior authorisation required for amounts over $300.

Hospitalisation

In-patient ward cover, theatre fees, anaesthesia, and nursing. Emergency admissions don't require pre-authorisation.

Dental

Preventive checkups, fillings, extractions, and root canal treatment. Orthodontics available on Platinum plan only.

Optical

Eye tests, prescription glasses, and contact lenses. One pair of frames covered per calendar year.

Prescription drugs

Chronic and acute medication dispensed at GENFIN-accredited pharmacies. Biometric verification at dispensing for member safety.

What's not covered

Common exclusions

All GENFIN plans have a 3-month waiting period for pre-existing conditions. The following are excluded across all plans:

  • Cosmetic or elective procedures not medically indicated
  • Experimental treatments not approved by a recognised health authority
  • Self-inflicted injuries
  • Injuries sustained while under the influence of alcohol or controlled substances
  • War, civil unrest, or nuclear events

Refer to your plan schedule for the full list. Questions? Chat with our claims team below.
