What GENFIN covers

Benefits across every stage of your healthcare journey.

Primary care

  • Unlimited GP consultations (Gold & Platinum)
  • Annual wellness checkups
  • Chronic disease management
  • Preventive health screenings
  • Nurse practitioner visits

Specialist & hospital

  • Specialist referrals (20+ disciplines)
  • In-patient hospitalisation
  • Theatre & anaesthesia fees
  • ICU / high-care cover
  • Oncology (Platinum only)

Pharmacy & medication

  • Chronic medication dispensing
  • Acute prescription drugs
  • Over-the-counter essentials (Platinum)
  • Biometric dispensing for safety
  • Home delivery option

Dental

  • 2 preventive checkups per year
  • Fillings & extractions
  • Root canal treatment
  • Dentures & prosthetics (Gold+)
  • Orthodontics (Platinum only)

Optical

  • Annual eye examination
  • Prescription frames (1/year)
  • Contact lenses
  • Specialist referral for glaucoma / cataracts

Mental health

  • Psychologist sessions (6/year, Gold+)
  • Psychiatrist consultations
  • Substance abuse counselling
  • 24/7 mental health helpline

Emergency & trauma

  • Emergency casualty cover (no pre-auth required)
  • Ambulance & emergency transport
  • Air evacuation (Platinum)
  • 24/7 emergency line

Family & maternity

  • Antenatal care (8 visits)
  • Hospital delivery cover
  • Postnatal follow-up
  • Child immunisations
  • Family members up to 4 dependants
