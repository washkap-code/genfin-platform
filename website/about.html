About GENFIN

One of Zimbabwe's most active healthcare funders

We're more than a health insurer — we're your health partner, covering over 60,000 lives across a growing portfolio of corporate and government clients.

GENFIN Medical Aid Fund is a health insurance company fully registered with the Ministry of Health and Child Welfare, and a member of the Association of Healthcare Funders of Zimbabwe (AHFOZ).

We specialise in managed health care, backed by a wide network of service providers and our own in-house optical, GP and dental services — so we can look after your care from consultation right through to medication.

We price every package to the specific needs of each client, so you get quality health care at the lowest cost possible, without incurring major costs. That commitment extends to our communities through free health and wellness programs for government and corporate clients.
