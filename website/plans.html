Good to know

Frequently asked questions

How do I join GENFIN?

Choose a plan and request a quote online, or call us on +263 78 632 3131. Civil servants can join by SSB payroll deduction (Pioneer $16, Premium $21, Prestige $26 per month), and employer groups receive a tailored proposal. Once you're a member you'll get access to the online member portal to manage everything in one place.

Are there waiting periods?

Yes. Optical benefits — consultation and in-house spectacles — become accessible after one month of membership, and some benefits require a minimum of 12 months' membership. Full terms are confirmed on your plan schedule.

Where do you deliver medication?

We deliver chronic medication nationwide — to homes, schools and companies across Zimbabwe — with live driver tracking and PIN-confirmed delivery, all visible in the member portal.

Can I add my family?

Yes. Every scheme already covers 2 people, and you can add extra dependants monthly: $5 on Vital, $10 on Gold, $15 on Diamond — and on SSB schemes just $1.00 (Pioneer), $1.50 (Premium) or $2.00 (Prestige) per dependant. Add them during onboarding or later from the member portal.

What does “managed health care” mean?

We coordinate your care across our provider network and in-house clinic — from consultation through to medication — so your care is joined up and you avoid major out-of-pocket costs.
