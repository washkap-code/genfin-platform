New to GENFIN? Start here

GENFIN Medical Aid Fund provides managed health care across Zimbabwe — GP and dental care, in-house optical, chronic medication delivered nationwide, and a wide provider network.

Choosing a plan

  1. Open Plans and compare Essential (from $29/month), Comprehensive (from $59/month) and Corporate (custom, for employer groups).
  2. Every package is finally priced to your needs — use the quote form on the Plans page and our team confirms your exact cover and premium.
  3. Employer groups: see For employers for group billing, reporting and a dedicated account manager.

Questions before joining

  • Benefits and limits by plan: Benefits.
  • Which doctors and pharmacies you can use: Providers.
  • Anything else: Support, or ask the GENFIN Assistant on any page.

Member portal guide

Signing in

  1. Click Portal login on the website (or go to /login), enter your email and password, and you land on your dashboard.
  2. Your dashboard shows your plan, your annual benefit usage bar, quick actions, your orders and your messages.

Ordering medication or optical items

  1. Open Order meds & optical. Every item shows live stock.
  2. Add items with the + button and press Place order.
  3. The platform instantly confirms stock, raises your invoice, books a delivery driver and gives you a delivery date.
  4. Pay from the banner on your dashboard (Pay now). Your receipt arrives in Messages — portal alert plus an email copy — and your benefit usage updates.

Pre-authorisation

  1. Open Pre-authorisation, choose the category (specialist consultation, hospitalisation, specialised dental, optical beyond limit, chronic registration or other).
  2. Enter the provider, the procedure and the estimated cost — your remaining benefit shows at the top.
  3. Submit. You get a reference (PA-…), and the decision — with the assessor's note — arrives in your Messages and email. Track everything in "My requests".

Documents & family

  • Membership certificate — official, QR-verified; print or save as PDF.
  • Benefit schedule — your full 2026 limits by category.
  • My profile & family — your details and registered dependants.

Staff: getting started

  1. New staff: choose Create your staff account on the sign-in page. Enter your full personal details, position and proposed employment date, and choose a password.
  2. Your account activates once the Head of HR approves it and assigns your role. Your role decides which modules you see.
  3. My profile holds your personal record, HR history, proof-of-employment letter and payslips — all downloadable on official letterhead.

HR & payroll module

Approving new staff

  1. Pending applications appear at the top of the HR screen with the applicant's full details.
  2. Assign a role (this controls their access), confirm the employment date, and press Approve. The approval — who and when — is recorded permanently in the HR log.

Payroll

  1. Press Run payroll for [month]. Payslips are issued to every approved staff member and the payroll journal posts to the ledger (DR 6000 Payroll / CR 1000 Cash).
  2. Staff download their own payslips from My profile.

Offboarding

  1. In the Offboarding panel, select the lawful reason for termination — the dropdown is aligned to the Labour Act [Chapter 28:01] and names the due process each ground presumes. Complete and document that process first.
  2. Set the last day of employment and confirm. Access is restricted immediately; the former staff member keeps payslip downloads and their letter becomes a Work Reference Letter (dates and role only — the reason is never stated on it).

Finance & accounts module

  • The KPI row shows Cash at Bank, Receivables, Inventory value and the trial balance check — it must always read "Balanced".
  • Invoices are raised automatically when members order. Record a direct payment with Record payment; member portal payments post themselves.
  • Every posting is double-entry and visible in the Journal — invoice (DR AR / CR Sales), payment (DR Cash / CR AR), cost of sales (DR COGS / CR Inventory), payroll (DR Payroll / CR Cash).

Inventory module

  • Live stock for pharmacy and optical with reorder alerts; the stock value always matches ledger account 1200.
  • Stock deducts automatically when an order is paid; every movement is logged with its invoice reference for audit.

Logistics & driver module

  • The driver register shows every registered driver with vehicle and live availability. Orders book the next available driver automatically.
  • Staff press Dispatch to start a run; the driver presses Mark delivered on the Driver app, which frees them for the next booking and notifies the member.

Enterprise dashboard (administrators)

  • Realtime activity — every event in every module, with actor and timestamp, refreshing automatically.
  • Pre-authorisations awaiting decision — approve or decline member requests directly; the member is notified instantly.
  • Who is logged in — all sessions with role and last-seen; a green dot means active in the last two minutes. This is your staff monitoring view.
  • KPIs: cash at bank, unpaid invoices, stock alerts, staff online now.

BioVerify — biometric identity

  • Enrolment (/bioverify-enrol): 4 steps — person and consent, fingerprints, face capture (uses your device camera — allow access when asked), confirm.
  • Verification (/biometric/verify.html): the counter screen used before dispensing.
  • QR check: scan the QR on any membership certificate to confirm it is valid.
  • In this environment the fingerprint scan is simulated; production adds certified USB scanners. No raw images are stored; consent is recorded at enrolment.

Troubleshooting

  • A staff or portal link sends me to the sign-in page. That page is protected — sign in with an account whose role has access. This is access control, not a broken link.
  • My account says "awaiting HR approval". The Head of HR has not yet approved your application; you will be able to sign in as soon as they do.
  • I see old content. Refresh the page (Ctrl+Shift+R / Cmd+Shift+R once); the platform now always serves the latest version.
  • Camera does not start on BioVerify. Allow camera access in the browser prompt, and check the page address begins with https.
  • Language. Use the EN / SN / ND switch at the bottom-left of any page for English, chiShona or isiNdebele.
  • Anything else — ask the GENFIN Assistant (bottom-right), or contact Support: +263 867 700 4400 · info@genfin.health.
