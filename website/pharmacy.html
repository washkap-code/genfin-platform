Digital prescription history

Collecting your medication

Secure, simple, and takes under 30 seconds at the counter.

01

Tell the pharmacist your name

The pharmacist enters your GENFIN member ID or searches by name to pull your prescription from the system.

02

Place your finger on the reader

A USB fingerprint reader on the counter verifies your identity against your enrolled biometric in under 2 seconds. No card, no PIN, no paperwork.

03

Collect & go

The system authorises dispensing, records the transaction, and sends you a digital receipt. Your full prescription history is visible in your member portal.

Chronic medication

Never run out of your regular medication

GENFIN manages chronic medication for over 18,000 members. Your monthly dispensing cycle is tracked automatically — when your supply is due for renewal, you'll receive a reminder to visit any accredited pharmacy.

Chronic conditions covered include hypertension, diabetes (Type 1 & 2), asthma, epilepsy, HIV/AIDS, thyroid disorders, and cardiovascular disease. Your GP registers your chronic script directly into the GENFIN system — no paper copies to carry.
