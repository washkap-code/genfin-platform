Frequently asked questions

How do I submit a claim?

Log in to your member portal, click "Submit claim", choose the claim type, upload your receipt or invoice photo, and submit. Most claims are processed within 24 hours.

What is biometric dispensing?

Instead of a membership card, you place your enrolled fingerprint on a reader at the pharmacy counter. The system verifies your identity in under 2 seconds and authorises dispensing of your prescription. This prevents fraud and ensures medication safety.

How do I enrol my fingerprint?

Visit any accredited GENFIN pharmacy or our Harare head office. A staff member will guide you through a 3-scan enrolment process that takes under 5 minutes. You'll need your member ID and a form of photo ID.

Can I add dependants to my plan?

Yes. Gold and Platinum plans cover up to 4 dependants (spouse and children under 21, or 25 if in full-time education). Add dependants through the member portal or by calling our membership team.

How long does claim payment take?

Approved claims are paid within 48 hours to your registered bank account or EcoCash wallet. Emergency claims at accredited facilities are settled directly with the provider — you pay nothing upfront.

What is a pre-existing condition waiting period?

All plans have a 3-month waiting period before claims can be made for conditions you had before joining GENFIN. Accidents and emergencies are covered from day one regardless.

Do I need a referral to see a specialist?

Yes — a GP referral is required for specialist claims on Gold and Platinum plans. Emergency specialist visits (e.g. at casualty) do not require prior referral. Claims without a referral for non-emergency specialists will not be processed.

Can I use GENFIN at any hospital?

For planned admissions, you must use an accredited GENFIN hospital to get the full benefit. For emergencies, you can attend any registered hospital and claim afterwards — we settle directly with the provider once you notify us within 24 hours of admission.

How do I cancel my membership?

Contact our membership team by phone or email. You may cancel at any time with 30 days' notice. Employer-sponsored memberships must be cancelled through your HR department.

Is my biometric data safe?

Yes. GENFIN stores only the derived fingerprint template (a mathematical representation of ridge patterns) — never the raw image. Templates are encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). Data is processed under Zimbabwe's Data Protection Act. You can request deletion of your biometric data at any time.
