How it works

Three steps.
Under two seconds.

BioVerify Facial integrates seamlessly with existing pharmacy, clinic, and hospital workflows — no specialist hardware required.

01
🔍
Member ID Lookup
Staff enter the member's ID number or phone number to load their profile. The system retrieves the biometric reference photo stored securely in the GENFIN cloud.
02
📸
Live Face Capture
The camera captures a live frame. An AI engine extracts 128-dimensional facial landmarks and liveness checks ensure it is not a photo or screen replay.
03
✅
Match & Authorise
The system compares vectors and returns a confidence score. Above 80% → green light. Below threshold → staff override with audit trail. All events are logged immutably.

FAQ

Common questions

What hardware do I need to run BioVerify Facial? ▼
Any standard webcam (720p or above), a tablet, or a dedicated kiosk terminal. We also supply purpose-built BioVerify terminals (ruggedised Android with integrated camera) if you prefer a turnkey solution. No specialist iris or fingerprint scanners required.
Is our members' biometric data stored at the pharmacy? ▼
No. Raw camera frames are processed in real time and immediately discarded. The encrypted biometric vector template lives only in the GENFIN secure cloud vault. Point-of-care terminals hold no biometric data.
What happens if the system can't verify a member? ▼
If the confidence score falls below the 80% threshold, the system presents a staff override option. The override is logged with timestamp, operator ID, and reason code — creating a complete audit trail for compliance review.
Does it work for dependants, not just the primary member? ▼
Yes. All listed dependants on a plan can enrol their biometric profile via the GENFIN member portal. Staff can select "verify dependant" and choose the covered person's name before scanning.
Is BioVerify Facial POPIA compliant? ▼
Yes. GENFIN processes biometric data under explicit informed consent obtained at member onboarding. Data is stored under AES-256 encryption and accessed only for verification purposes. Full data-subject rights are respected, including the right to erasure.
Can we use BioVerify independently of the GENFIN medical aid system? ▼
Yes — BioVerify Facial is offered as a standalone SaaS product. You can enrol your own user base via our admin portal or API and use the system for any identity-verification workflow: banking KYC, exam centres, logistics access, and more.
